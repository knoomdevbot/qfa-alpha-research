## Alpha Hypothesis
- Claim: Timestamped official antitrust merger-review events from FTC/DOJ sources may create post-event drift or reversal in U.S.-listed target/acquirer common stocks, but only if a conservative official event table can be built without company-PR hindsight or current-ticker survivorship bias.

They can alter deal-completion probability, spread compression, acquirer financing/strategic expectations, and sector peer risk.
