## Alpha Hypothesis
- **Claim:** Liquid sector/industry/theme ETFs with large Oct-Nov/December drawdowns rebound around late December / early January as tax-loss selling exhausts and January reinvestment/re-risking begins.
- **Economic / behavioral intuition:** Tax-loss selling and year-end de-risking may create temporary pressure in visible loser ETFs; new-year reinvestment can reverse some pressure.
- **Expected failure mode:** The effect is a generic January/turn-of-month/risk-on or short-term-reversal premium rather than loser-specific, or public daily bars are too sparse/noisy to isolate it.
